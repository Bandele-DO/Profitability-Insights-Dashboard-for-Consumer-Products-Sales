# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard

## 🧾 Overview
This project presents an interactive Sales Performance Dashboard built to analyze the performance of a retail business selling **Electronics, Furniture, and Home Appliances**.

The dashboard highlights key metrics such as Revenue, COGS, Profit, and Customer Trends to help stakeholders understand performance drivers and make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Monitor overall business performance  
- Identify top and low performing products  
- Evaluate sales representatives’ contributions  
- Understand regional revenue and cost patterns  
- Detect trends and anomalies in customer data  

---

## 🧭 Business Context
The business operates across multiple regions and product categories, generating large volumes of sales data.  
Leadership needs visibility into performance metrics to optimize strategy, allocate resources effectively, and improve profitability.

---

## ❓ Key Business Question
Which products, regions, and sales representatives contribute the most to profitability, and where should the company focus its efforts to maximize growth?

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- KPI cards for Revenue, Profit, COGS, and Customers  
- Profit by Product analysis  
- Revenue by Sales Representative  
- COGS by City comparison  
- Monthly Customer Trend  
- Region and Category filters  

---

## 🛠 Tools
- Excel  
- Power Query  
- Pivot Tables and Charts

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- The monthly customer trend showed a sharp drop in **May**, making it the lowest-performing month.  
- After investigating the dataset, May contained **only a single recorded day**, indicating a **data completeness issue rather than a true performance decline**.  
- This highlights the importance of validating data quality before drawing conclusions from visualizations.  

---

## 📌 Recommendations
- Implement data validation checks to ensure complete monthly records  
- Monitor data collection processes to prevent gaps  
- Use the dashboard for ongoing performance tracking and strategic planning  

---

## 🚀 Outcome
The dashboard provides a clear and interactive view of business performance, enabling stakeholders to quickly identify opportunities, detect anomalies, and make informed decisions.
